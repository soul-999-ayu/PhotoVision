Ophthalmic equipment plays a vital role in examining, diagnosing, and treating eye conditions. Instruments such as slit lamps for comprehensive eye examinations, tonometers for measuring intraocular pressure, and ophthalmoscopes for viewing internal eye structures are essential in clinical practice.

Additionally, keratometers assess corneal curvature, while autorefractors assist in determining accurate eyeglass prescriptions. These indispensable tools support the ongoing maintenance of ocular health and facilitate early detection and treatment of vision-related disorders. Ophthalmic equipment is crucial for ensuring long-term visual clarity and comprehensive eye care.
